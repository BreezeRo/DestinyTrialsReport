Partnership with Guardian.gg!

  •  November 6th, 2015

We have recently made some friends over at Guardian.gg, they love to collect all kinds of data and agreed to let us use anything we want to make our site more useful for you.

All they want in exchange is for you to visit and let them know what kind of data and content you would like to see more of.

Right now nothing much has changed, we have replaced the Bungie rating with a custom Elo rating that is calculated in-house by Guardian.gg

We are planning some big updates coming up so stay tuned and good luck this week Guardians!

As always, if you have any suggestions for us, or if you've found a bug, please feel free to send us a message. Our contact info is below. See everyone soon!

If you come across any issue, error, or just want to send us a suggestion you can email us or open a ticket on GitHub.
