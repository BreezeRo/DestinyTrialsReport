Charity of the week

Support charity and get a badge of your choice on your player card, for example Philantropist, Saintor Hero! This is how it works:

  1. We pick a different charity each week
  2. You donate an amount of at least $50 through PayPal
    • We donate the entire amount minus $10 to the charity of the week
    • Why the large amount? We want to make sure a custom badge is something special without polluting every card. This is a pilot though, so we might tweak the amount in the future.
  3. Along with your donation you pick
    • The word you want for your badge (maximum of 14 characters)
    • A color you like
  4. We check whether your badge meets all our requirements.
    We have spent some time on the internet, this is to filter out stuff like Dickbutt
  5. Give us some time to implement your custom badge and show it to aaall your friends! You will most likely have it by the next Trials weekend.
