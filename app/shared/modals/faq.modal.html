Is there a Destiny Trials Report app?

Not for the time being, but you can achieve a native app feeling by adding this webpage to your homescreen.

Why did my flawless count not update?

Flawless count is based on lighthouse visits per character per week, this is the only way we cna do it.

Why don't you support other game modes like Skirmish, for example?

Bungie does not let us see the current fireteam you're matched with. We're considering it, but it would likely require manual entry of player names.

What data do you store on your servers?

We store only Trials data, this includes individual players, the matches they played and weapons they have killed with
