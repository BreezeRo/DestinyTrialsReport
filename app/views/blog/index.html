Introducing: myDestinyTrialsReport

  • 11th Aug 2015

Since we have seen a lot of interest in sharing your own stats through our app we have made it a little bit easier for you.

my.DestinyTrialsReport.com works just like our fireteam search but instead of partnering you up with your recent trials teammates we group you with your other characters.

Navigate to http://my.DestinyTrialsReport.com/ and use the search.

If you come across any issue, error, or just want to send us a suggestion you can email us or open a ticket on GitHub.
